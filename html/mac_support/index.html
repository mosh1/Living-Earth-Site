General

Open Living Earth on Login

To have Living Earth open automatically on login, visit 'Preferences', under the 'General' tab, check the box titled 'Start Living Earth at Login'. To turn this feature off, you will need to return to preferences and uncheck this box.

Bright Night Clouds

When viewing the weather of an area at night it can sometimes be difficult to see the details of the cloud layer. You can turn on 'Bright Night Clouds' to artificially brighten the clouds on the shadow side of the earth. To do this, click on the 'Gear' icon in Living Earth, and from the drop down menu select 'Bright Night Clouds'. Optionally, when Living Earth is open, you can use the hot key 'Command-B'.

Menu Bar

Living Earth Desktop is a menu bar app. All of Living Earth features are accessible from your menu bar, including closing Living Earth and setting your preferences. You can easily access Living Earth with a hotkey which is defaulted to Option-W. You can configure a different key combination in the Living Earth preferences.

Icon and Temperature

The weather condition icon and temperature displayed in your menu bar is the first city in your list. You can change this at any time by clicking the 'plus' icon, then click and drag any city up to the top of the list.

Screensaver

NOTE: Living Earth is not a system screensaver. Apps sold in the Mac App store are not allowed to install system screen savers. Because of this limitation, the Living Earth screensaver cannot password lock your computer.

Explore Mode

In 'explore' mode, the screensaver will randomly visit interesting cities around the globe, including any city in your list. Many of these cities have either extreme weather (the hottest and coldest places on earth) or are capitals of countries both large and small. Explore mode can be both fun and informative as you discover new places you've heard of but never looked up where they are on a globe!

Settings For Best Results

For best results using the Living Earth Screensaver, you should set your monitor to sleep after Living Earth activates. You can do this in the OSX preferences panel titled 'Energy Saver'. You should also disable your system screensaver found in the OSX preference panel titled 'Desktop & Screensaver'.
