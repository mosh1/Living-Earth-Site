Sending Crash Reports

If Living Earth has crashed on your device for any reason, you can send us a crash report by extracting the report from your device. The crash report helps us resolve your issue and find a solution so that it does not happen again.

iOS 8

1. Select your device Settings
2. Select Privacy
3. Scroll down to Diagnostics & Usage
4. Select Diagnostic & Usage Data
5. Tap and hold the most recent crash report under com.radiantlabs.Earth-Clock
6. Select and copy all of the text
7. Paste the text into an email to support@radiantlabs.io and send


iOS 7

1. Select your device Settings
2. Select General
3. Select About
4. Scroll down to Diagnostics & Usage
5. Select Diagnostic & Usage Data
6. Tap and hold the most recent crash report under com.radiantlabs.Earth-Clock
7. Select and copy all of the text
8. Paste the text into an email to support@radiantlabs.io and send
